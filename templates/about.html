How to upload your Recipes

  • First have you registered? Sign up now to upload your favorite recipes
  • Log-in with your username and password
  • click on Add recipes
  • Add your Recipe name and Choose a category
  • Select the Prep time which is how long it takes to prepare your ingredients before cooking
  • Select your Cooking time which is how long the recipe takes to cook or bake
  • Choose a difficuly level - is it an easy recipe or something that a prochef has made
  • Now comes the fun!! Add your ingredients with their Quantities then type out your instructions
  • Please make your Instructions as detailed as possible and check your grammer and spell check before submitting
  • Have more instructions to add no problem!! Just click add instruction field and another textbox will appear to add those extra secret moves
  • You can add a link to your recipe image to if you have shared it on instagram or facebook
  • Then submit your recipe!!
  • It will appear on the top of the Home page but you can also veiw it in your Profile

How to Edit or Delete your Recipes

  • Log-in with your username and password
  • You will be redirected to your Profile
  • Choose your Recipe name and Click edit
  • You will be directed to the editing page where you can update or change your recipe thatyou have added
  • Then click the edit submit button to update your recipe!!
  • It will appear in your Profile for you to veiw later
  • Be careful when clicking delete recipe it will automatically delete your recipe from your profile
